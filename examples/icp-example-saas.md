# ICP & Personas Example — B2B SaaS (Marketing Analytics)

Copy this into `.claude/skills/icp/SKILL.md` and adjust to your business.

---

## Ideal Customer Profile (Company Level)

- **Industry**: B2B SaaS, FinTech, Professional Services
- **Company size**: 50–500 employees, €2M–30M ARR
- **Geography**: DACH, CEE, Western Europe, MEA (Middle East)
- **Tech stack signals**: Uses HubSpot or Salesforce, runs paid acquisition, has 2–10 person marketing team
- **Buying trigger**: Scaling demand gen, preparing for Series A/B, replacing spreadsheet-based reporting
- **Disqualifiers**: Pre-revenue startups, B2C, no dedicated marketing function

---

## Persona 1: Head of Marketing

- **Job title(s)**: Head of Marketing, VP Marketing, Director of Demand Gen
- **Reports to**: CMO or CEO
- **Core KPIs**: MQLs, pipeline generated, marketing-sourced revenue, CAC
- **Top 3 pain points**:
  1. Can't attribute pipeline to specific campaigns
  2. Reporting takes hours of spreadsheet work each month
  3. CEO keeps asking "is marketing working?" and there's no clear answer
- **Desired outcome**: Automated dashboards, clear attribution, prove marketing ROI
- **Objections**: "We already have Google Analytics", "Our team is too busy to onboard a new tool"
- **Language they use**: "pipeline", "attribution", "demand gen", "MQL", "campaign performance"
- **Where they search**: Google ("marketing attribution tool"), G2, LinkedIn, peer Slack communities
- **Emotional driver**: Wants to be seen as a revenue driver, not a cost center

### Ad messaging hints
- Lead with ROI proof and time savings
- Social proof from similar-size B2B companies
- Low-commitment CTAs: "See a live demo", "Get the benchmark report"

---

## Persona 2: CEO / Founder

- **Job title(s)**: CEO, Co-Founder, Managing Director
- **Reports to**: Board, investors
- **Core KPIs**: ARR growth, burn rate, revenue per employee
- **Top 3 pain points**:
  1. Marketing spend feels opaque — unclear what's working
  2. Needs growth but doesn't want to hire more marketers
  3. Board wants data-driven justification for marketing budget
- **Desired outcome**: One dashboard showing marketing ROI, predictable pipeline
- **Objections**: "How quickly will I see results?", "We can't afford another tool right now"
- **Language they use**: "ROI", "growth", "efficiency", "revenue", "scale"
- **Where they search**: Google ("marketing ROI software"), LinkedIn thought leaders, CEO peer groups
- **Emotional driver**: Wants control and predictability, fears wasting runway

### Ad messaging hints
- Lead with business outcomes and speed to value
- Numbers beat features: "3x faster reporting" > "Customizable dashboards"
- Direct CTAs: "Calculate your ROI", "Book a 15-min strategy call"
