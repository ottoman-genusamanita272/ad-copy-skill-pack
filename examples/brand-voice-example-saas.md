# Brand Voice Example — B2B SaaS Company

Copy this file into `.claude/skills/brand-voice/SKILL.md` and adjust to your brand.

---

## Company
- **Name**: Acme Analytics
- **Industry**: B2B SaaS — Marketing Analytics Platform
- **Primary markets**: DACH, CEE

## Tone of Voice
- **Primary tone**: Confident expert — data-driven, no fluff
- **Avoid**: Buzzwords without substance, aggressive sales push, overly casual tone
- **Key adjectives**: Precise, actionable, transparent, scalable

## Messaging Hierarchy
1. **Primary**: Marketing analytics that turns data into pipeline
2. **Secondary**: Single platform for attribution, forecasting, and ROI reporting
3. **Tertiary**: Trusted by 200+ B2B companies across Europe

## Social Proof
- "200+ B2B companies"
- "3x faster reporting"
- "HubSpot & Salesforce certified integration"
- "SOC 2 Type II compliant"

## CTAs
- EN: "Start Free Trial", "See It in Action", "Get a Demo"
- DE: "Kostenlos testen", "Demo ansehen", "Jetzt starten"
- FR: "Essayez gratuitement", "Voir la démo", "Commencez maintenant"
- ES: "Prueba gratis", "Ver demostración", "Empieza ahora"
- AR: "جرّب مجاناً", "شاهد العرض", "ابدأ الآن"
- PL: "Przetestuj za darmo", "Zobacz demo", "Sprawdź pricing"

## Language Notes
- EN ads: American spelling
- DE ads: "Sie" form (formal, professional)
- FR ads: "vous" form (formal, B2B)
- ES ads: "usted" form (formal, B2B); Spain (ES-ES) vocabulary
- AR ads: Modern Standard Arabic (MSA); brand names stay in Latin script
- PL ads: "Ty" form (informal, direct)
