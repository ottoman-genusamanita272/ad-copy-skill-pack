### Ad messaging hints for this persona
- Headlines that work: benefit-driven (ROI, speed), social proof (peer companies)
- Headlines that don't: feature-heavy, jargon they don't use
- CTA preference: low commitment ("See how", "Get the guide") over high commitment ("Buy now")

- **Core KPIs**: [e.g., Revenue growth, burn rate, time to profitability]

- **Desired outcome**: [e.g., Predictable, scalable growth without growing headcount]

- **Where they search**: [e.g., Google, LinkedIn thought leadership, peer CEO groups]

### Ad messaging hints for this persona
- Headlines that work: outcome-driven (revenue, growth), urgency, numbers
- Headlines that don't: tactical details, marketing jargon
- CTA preference: results-oriented ("See ROI calculator", "Book a strategy call")
